Sharing what I learnt as a music artist

Notes from the Indie Path.
  • As a new artist, how would you suggest me to promote my music?

    Based on my experience, leveraging platforms like LenosTube can be effective. Additionally, utilize social media, collaborate with influencers, submit to music blogs/playlists, and perform live or virtually.

  • As a new artist, how can I distribute my music on all music platforms?

    As a new artist, using services like dittomusic can help distribute your music across all major platformslike Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, and more. Simply upload your tracks, and Ditto Music takes care of the distribution process for you, making it easier to reach a wider audience.

  • How do you stay inspired as a music artist?

    I find inspiration in everyday moments, emotions, and experiences. It's important to stay true to yourself and your unique vision.

  • What advice do you have for aspiring indie musicians?

    Never stop creating, experimenting, and pushing boundaries. Surround yourself with supportive people, stay open to feedback, and never lose sight of your passion.
